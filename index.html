the artists

Jennifer Steinkamp

Jennifer Steinkamp is an American installation artist who is best known for her large-scale, animated video projections. Her work often explores the relationship between technology and nature, as well as the human body and space.

Cindy Sherman

Cindy Sherman is an American photographer and filmmaker who is best known for her conceptual self-portraits. In her work, she often explores the construction of identity and gender.

Julian Schnabel

Julian Schnabel is an American painter and film director who is best known for his abstract expressionist paintings. He has also directed several films, including the biopic Basquiat.

Paul Prudhomme

Paul Prudhomme was a French-American chef who was best known for his Cajun and Creole cuisine. He was also a pioneer in the use of exotic spices and flavors in his dishes.

Robert Longo

Robert Longo is an American painter and sculptor who is best known for his large-scale black-and-white drawings. His work often addresses social and political issues, such as violence, war, and consumerism.
